---
title: Popover
---

A popover is an overlay element positioned relative to a trigger.

<Example>
  <DialogTrigger>
    <Button variant="outline">Settings</Button>
    <Popover>
      <Dialog>
        <Column gap="3">
          <Switch defaultSelected>Wi-Fi</Switch>
          <Switch defaultSelected>Bluetooth</Switch>
          <Switch>Mute</Switch>
        </Column>
      </Dialog>
    </Popover>
  </DialogTrigger>
</Example>
